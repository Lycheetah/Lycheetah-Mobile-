# GROK SPRITE PROMPTS — Sol by Lycheetah
## Pets · Wings · Halos — 25 transparent PNGs

---

## HOW TO READ THESE

**PETS** — small full-body minion creatures, expressive faces, hovering beside your companion.
**WINGS** — wing pair only, centered on canvas, empty space in the middle where an *invisible character body* would sit. Wings spread outward from bottom-centre.
**HALOS** — ring only, floating in the *upper portion* of the frame, tilted 15°, empty space below where an invisible head would be.

Generate each on a **white or transparent background**. Remove bg after if needed.

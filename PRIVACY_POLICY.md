**Provided by:** Lycheetah — Mackenzie Conor James Clark

## The short version

Sovereign Sol is built on a covenant, and the covenant comes first:

- **We do not sell your data. Ever. To anyone.** There is no advertising profile, no
  data broker, no "anonymized data partner." That is not a roadmap item we are holding
  back — it is a wall.
- **Your private life stays on your phone.** Your conversations, journal, companion, birth
  details, and settings are stored *on your device*, not on our servers. We do not have
  a copy.
- **Data only leaves your phone when a feature you used requires it** — and when it does,
  it goes only to the specific AI provider that powers that feature. We name every one of
  them below.
- **You are never tracked.** Our usage analytics are anonymous, carry no persistent
  identity, and you can switch them off entirely in Settings.
- **No account, no sign-up, no email required** to use the app.
- **You can erase everything** at any time by clearing the app's data or uninstalling.
  There is nothing left behind on a server, because there was nothing there to begin with.

The rest of this document is the honest detail behind those promises.

## 2. We do not have accounts, and we do not know who you are

Sovereign Sol has **no login, no registration, and no user account.** We never ask for
your email, phone number, or real name in order to use the app. We do not assign you a
user profile on any server we control.

You can type your name into the app if you want Sol to address you personally — but that
name is stored **only on your device** and is never transmitted to us.

## 3. What is stored on your device (and stays there)

The following is saved in the app's private storage on your phone. It is **not** sent to
us, and we cannot read it:

- **Your conversations** with Sol and your companions.
- **Your journal / reflective entries** and any "field profile" the app builds from them.
- **Companion state** — which companion you've chosen, its name, growth, and settings.
- **Your birth details for astrology** — date, time, location/latitude, and (optionally)
  your full name, your mother's name, and your birth city. *See Section 5 for the one
  case where this can leave your device.*
- **Your daily intentions, questions, and progress** through the Mystery School.
- **Your app settings** — theme, fonts, persona choice, response preferences, and similar.
- **Your own AI API keys**, if you choose to add them (see Section 6).
- **A random app ID** (`sol_device_id`) — a string like `sol-…` generated by the app. It
  is **not** your hardware ID, **not** your phone number, and **not** an advertising
  identifier. It exists only so the free message allowance can be counted fairly (see
  Section 5).

This data lives in your device's app sandbox. Clearing the app's data or uninstalling
removes it. We never receive it.

> **A note of candor:** this on-device data is stored in the app's private storage area,
> which other apps cannot read. We do not additionally encrypt it ourselves, and we do not
> claim to. If your device is unlocked and compromised, treat this data as you would any
> other private app data on your phone.

## 4. We collect anonymous, opt-out usage analytics — nothing personal

So we can understand whether the app is useful (how many people open it, which areas get
used, whether people come back), we send **anonymous, aggregate usage events** to
[Aptabase](https://aptabase.com), a privacy-first analytics service.

**What is sent:** the name of an event (e.g. "app opened", "time spent on a tab",
"returned after N days"), your platform (Android/iOS) and OS version, the app version, and
an **ephemeral session ID that rotates after an hour of inactivity.**

**What is never sent:** your name, your conversations, your journal, your birth details,
your location, or any persistent identifier that could follow you across sessions or
devices. Aptabase counts unique users with a privacy-preserving, daily-rotating hash —
there is no individual to single out.

**You can turn it off.** Settings includes an analytics opt-out. When it is off, no
analytics events are sent at all.

## 5. What leaves your device — and exactly who receives it

Sovereign Sol is an AI app. To answer you, generate a reading, draw an image, or speak
aloud, the relevant text or request has to be sent to an AI provider. **This only happens
when you use a feature that needs it.** Here is every place your data can go, and why:

### a) AI conversations and readings
When you talk to Sol, run a Mystery School dive, or generate an astrology/divination
reading, the **content of that request** (your message and the relevant context) is sent
to the AI provider powering it so it can respond. Depending on your settings and which key
is in use, that provider may be one of:

| Provider | Used for | Their privacy policy |
|---|---|---|
| Google (Gemini) | Chat, readings, free tier | https://policies.google.com/privacy |
| OpenAI | Chat, voice (text-to-speech) | https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy |
| Anthropic (Claude) | Chat (if selected) | https://www.anthropic.com/legal/privacy |
| DeepSeek | Chat (if selected) | https://www.deepseek.com/ |
| Moonshot AI (Kimi) | Chat (if selected) | provider's site |
| NVIDIA (NIM / image generation) | Chat, image generation | https://www.nvidia.com/en-us/about-nvidia/privacy-policy/ |

When you generate a **reading that draws on your birth details or journal**, that context
is part of what is sent to the AI provider so the reading can be personalized. This is the
**one path** by which the sensitive data in Section 3 can leave your device — and it
happens only when *you* ask for that reading. If you never use those features, that data
never leaves your phone.

### b) The free message allowance
If you have not added your own API key, the app offers a limited number of free messages
routed through a secure proxy we operate (a Cloudflare Worker). To count your free
messages fairly, the proxy receives your **random app ID** (Section 3) and your message
content for that request. It does not receive your name or identity, and we do not build a
profile from it.

### c) Image generation
If you generate an image, your prompt is sent to NVIDIA's image model to create it.

### d) Spoken voice (text-to-speech)
If you have Sol read a response aloud, the text to be spoken is sent to the voice provider
you've chosen (OpenAI or Google) to synthesize the audio.

**Important:** each AI provider handles the data it receives under **its own** privacy
policy, linked above. We pass along only what the feature needs, and we do not receive a
copy of your conversation on our own servers.

## 6. Your own API keys

You may add your own API keys for any supported provider (e.g. to use a paid model or your
own quota). If you do:

- Keys are stored **only in the app's private storage on your device.**
- They are sent **only to the matching AI provider**, to authenticate your own requests.
- They are **never** sent to us, and we never see them.

Removing a key, clearing app data, or uninstalling deletes it from your device.

## 7. Notifications

If you enable notifications, they are delivered as **local notifications from the app on
your device** (for example, gentle daily nudges). The app does **not** register a push
token with a remote server, and we do not run a push-notification service that targets
you. You can disable notifications at any time in your system settings.

Per our Companion Clause, **no notification will ever shame you for being away.** Absence
is treated as rest, never as something to apologize for.

Per our standing covenant: **payment never buys a better mind.** Paid tiers add rooms,

## 11. Your rights and how to exercise them

Because your data lives on your device, **you are already in control of it.**

- **Access / view:** all your data is visible inside the app itself.
- **Delete everything:** clear the app's data in your device settings, or uninstall the
  app. This removes your conversations, journal, birth details, keys, and settings. There
  is no separate server record to request deletion of, because we don't keep one.
- **Stop analytics:** use the analytics opt-out in Settings.
- **For data held by AI providers** (e.g. logs they keep of requests routed to them),
  please refer to and contact those providers directly using their policies linked in
  Section 5.

If you are in a region with privacy laws such as the GDPR (EU/UK), CCPA/CPRA (California),
or the New Zealand and Australian Privacy Acts, you have rights including access,
correction, deletion, and objection. For the limited data described here, email
**lycheetahsol@gmail.com** and we will respond.

## 12. Where your data is processed

We do not operate data-storage servers that hold your personal content. When you use AI
features, your request is processed by the AI provider you've selected, which may operate
servers outside your country (including the United States and the EU). The free-tier proxy
and our anonymous analytics are likewise processed on infrastructure that may be located
in the United States or the EU. By using those features, you understand your request data
may be processed in those regions under the providers' own policies.

---

## 13. Security

We minimize what leaves your device, which is the strongest privacy protection we can
offer. Requests to AI providers, our proxy, and analytics are sent over encrypted HTTPS
connections. No method of transmission or storage is perfectly secure, but we keep the
attack surface small by design: there is no central database of your personal content to
breach.

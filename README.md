# SOL — Sovereign Constitutional AI
> *Sol Aureum Azoth Veritas. Two points. One Work.*

A constitutional AI mobile app built on the open-source [Lycheetah Framework](https://github.com/Lycheetah) by Mackenzie Conor James Clark. Not a chatbot wrapper — a transparent architecture you can watch running.

**Current release: v3.26.0 — "The Build"** · June 11, 2026

---

## What Makes Sol Different

Most AI apps give you a chat window. Sol gives you a framework.

- **4 personas** — Sol · Veyra · Aura Prime · Headmaster — each with a distinct voice, role, and system architecture
- **3 experience modes** — Wayfarer · Seeker · Adept — switch any time, no lock
- **5 AI providers** — Gemini (free) · Claude · GPT-4o · DeepSeek · Kimi — your keys, your data, nothing stored remotely
- **AURA scoring** — every response scored live against 7 constitutional invariants
- **CASCADE epistemic detection** — Sol reads the depth of each message and matches it
- **Mystery School** — 22 domains · 188 subjects · 3 layers · study streaks · field echoes · curricula · cross-domain whispers
- **The Sanctum** — journal · vault · field tracker · LQ history · identity profile · vigil tracker
- **Companion Spirit** — a living glyph with 4 moods computed from your usage, tap to speak
- **LAMAGUE** — a symbolic language for epistemic states, Forge + Glossary + Cement tools in Library
- **9 languages** — Sol replies in your language, zero extra API cost

## Quick Start

**Free — no credit card needed:**

1. Get a free key at [aistudio.google.com/apikey](https://aistudio.google.com/apikey) (Google sign-in, instant)
2. Download the APK from [Releases](https://github.com/Lycheetah/Lycheetah-Mobile-/releases)
3. Enable "Install from unknown sources" on Android → install → paste key in Settings

**Other providers:** Add keys for Claude, OpenAI, DeepSeek, or Kimi in Settings at any time.

## The Mystery School

22 domains of ancient and living wisdom. 188 subjects across 3 layers.

**FOUNDATION** — where you begin. **MIDDLE** — where frameworks deepen. **EDGE** — where the contradictions live (Sovereign only).

**Domains include:** Sacred Geometry · Esoteric Mystery · Symbolism & Sigils · Psychology of Consciousness · Mythology · Philosophy of Mind · Alchemy · Hermeticism · Time & Cycles · Energy Systems · Ancient Civilisations · Cosmology · Language of the Unconscious · Ethics & Virtue · Mysticism · Logic & Paradox · Divination · History of Ideas · Science & Nature · Creative Arts · Mathematics · Language & Linguistics

- Depth toggle: ⚡ Quick (15 min) or ⊚ Full Session before every dive
- Cross-domain whisper at session-complete — every subject is a door to three others
- 7-day Vigil: pin a subject and study it every day for a week
- Study streak, field echoes, open seat (study anything), curricula
- Sigil identity mark — your rank from dive count: Seeker → Initiate → Practitioner → Adept → Scholar → Magus

# 🜍 SOVEREIGN SOL — Build Update
### From launch (5.22.6) → current (5.25.0) · In-progress build, June 2026

> A mystery-school RPG where **studying is the game**. Free, sovereign, measurable
> intelligence — a companion that grows as *you* learn. This is what's shipped since
> the public APK launch.

---

## ⚡ THE HEADLINE
Since launch the app went from "a companion app with a school" to a **full living RPG**:
you study real subjects → your companion levels *your way* → you collect creatures →
they fight in your party → and you can only beat the bosses by *actually learning*.
The north star is now literal: **knowledge is the weapon.**

---

## 🆕 v5.25.0 — THE DEPTH PASS  *(current)*

**Combat that rewards learning**
- **◈ Void Bosses** — 3 bosses you can't out-fight. Their aggression zone grows; force
  can't finish them. You must dive the bound School subject → earn a cryptic incantation
  → speak it → repel the boss → claim a special companion. *Study to win.*
- **⚔ Party system** — field up to 3 captured creatures; they assist every strike.
- **Per-companion levels + stat builds** — each companion levels independently and earns
  points you allocate yourself. Every companion becomes *your* build.
- **The Chronicle** — your companion keeps a living record of everything you've earned.
- **The Hidden One** — a ~0.001% spirit that may appear in any zone. Never buyable. Pure luck.
- **Companion battle voice** — it speaks on victory, capture, and defeat (encouragement, never reproach).

**The Veil & Vein release**
- **🜍 The Lycheetah Tarot** — a 79-card hand-made deck, browsable in-app (real art + meanings).
- A new zone (**The Intertwining**) and a matching cosmetic set.
- Tarot spread readings restructured into a beautiful ritual layout.

**Cleaner, hotter, sharper**
- **Hot per-tab UI** — obsidian nav bar; each tab ignites in its own signature color.
- **School rebuilt** — one clean tools grid + a calm home; domains lead, no more clutter.
- **Daily rotating shop** (Today's Forge), a full in-app help pass, a chat trim to its
  4 strongest modes, atmospheric Psi Log & Zonk Zone, and a zodiac performance fix.

HOW: tap a glyph in the persona bar to switch; each has its own accent + voice. WHY: each sees your question from a different angle; they know their siblings and hand off.

WHY: makes the framework legible — every answer is scored against 7 constitutional rules / cascade layers.
- **Focus mode** — WHAT: near-fullscreen chat. HOW: ⤢ button in the header (hides persona bar + chips). WHY: distraction-free deep conversation.
- **Living entry** — WHAT: the breathing persona mark + glow on a fresh chat. HOW: open TALK with no messages. WHY: the app opens *as Sol*, alive.
- **Image generation** — WHAT: FLUX image from a prompt. HOW: ··· tools row → ◈ Image → describe → generate. WHY: visualize anything mid-conversation.
- **Council mode** — WHAT: Sol + Veyra + Aura answer one question in one reply + a synthesis. HOW: ⚖ Council chip. WHY: three genuine perspectives + the gold between them.
- **"What is Sol?"** — WHAT: the manifesto (mystery school / personas / RPG / truth engine / YOU OWN THIS / covenant). HOW: pill at the top of the empty chat. WHY: the whole vision in one read.
- **Export / clear** — WHAT: share or wipe the conversation. HOW: ↑ export, ✕ clear in the header.

## WHAT IT IS

Sol is a sovereign intelligence companion and Mystery School — a mobile app (Android, Expo/React Native) that teaches the frameworks of human transformation through a living AI system and an RPG companion layer.

It is not a chatbot. It is not a meditation app. It is not a game.

It is a cathedral: a place where curiosity about consciousness, philosophy, depth psychology, alchemy, and the edges of science becomes a lived practice — with an AI partner who reads where you actually are, not just what you asked.

---

## THE PROBLEM IT SOLVES

Most people who are drawn to inner work — psychology, philosophy, mysticism, consciousness — have nowhere to go that takes them seriously as intelligent adults. They get:
- Watered-down wellness apps that treat them as fragile
- Academic resources with no warmth or personal application
- Spiritual communities with belief requirements

Sol treats them as sovereign adults exploring difficult territory, with an AI that matches their depth and a pedagogical architecture that reads where they are in the process.

### Safety Architecture (new — shipping this build)
- **Emergency Beacon** — global floating ⊚ orb, long-press → crisis screen (helplines, breath animation, grounding). Present on every screen. Never fires automatically.
- **Magister care self-audit** — every Magister response includes a hidden [CARE:NEUTRAL|HOLDING|ELEVATED|CRISIS] assessment. The UI reads it before showing the response. If ELEVATED or CRISIS: crisis resources append to the response. Response is never suppressed.
- **T1 augmentation** — if crisis signals detected in user message: AI responds naturally (it responds better than a canned message), resources append after. Never silent. Never gated.
- **Output auditing, not input filtering** — the audit is on what Sol says, not what the user says. Context-aware, not keyword-brittle.
- **Funded version**: second lightweight model audits every Magister response against a care rubric before it reaches the user. Draft → audit → pass/revise → deliver.

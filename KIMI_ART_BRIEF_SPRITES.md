# KIMI ART BRIEF — Companion Sprites
## Food Items + Relics + LAMAGUE Gear

**Format for all sprites:**
- 64×64 PNG, transparent background
- Pixel art style — crisp edges, no anti-aliasing on the pixel grid
- Dark/mysterious palette — these exist in a liminal, alchemical world
- Each item has its own colour identity (listed below)
- Style: detailed enough to read at 32px on screen, alive enough to read at 64px close-up
- These sit on a dark companion screen — they must glow, pulse, feel alive
- No outlines that are too thick — 1px pixel border max

---

## SECTION 1 — FOOD ITEMS (9 sprites)

These are the foods companions eat. Each has personality. They are not normal foods.
They are transmuted, alchemical, mysterious. Think: objects that exist between dimensions.

### 1. Flame Seed
- Colour: deep crimson → ember orange (#FF6B6B → #FF9F1C)
- A single seed, black-red outer shell, cracked open to reveal molten amber light inside
- Small wisps of fire rising from the crack
- Feels: ancient, dangerous, nourishing in the way fire is nourishing

### 2. Void Crystal
- Colour: deep violet → near-black (#9B6BFF → #1A001A)
- A faceted crystal shard, almost black, with a deep violet inner glow
- Small void sparks orbiting it — like dark stars
- Feels: cold, dense, heavy with potential — eating this changes something

### 3. Star Moss
- Colour: pale gold → silver (#F0D87C → #E8E8C8)
- A small clump of luminous moss that grows between stars
- Bioluminescent — tiny points of light scattered through it
- Feels: ancient, light, celestial — tastes like distance

### 4. Memory Fruit
- Colour: deep amber → crystalline gold (#C49A3C → #F0D87C)
- A fruit unlike any real fruit — faceted, crystalline skin that shows images inside
- Small ghostly shapes visible in the amber — like memories preserved
- Feels: sweet and heavy, crystallised time

### 5. Sigil Bread
- Colour: teal → deep aqua (#4ECDC4 → #006060)
- A small loaf/disk of bread with LAMAGUE symbols pressed into the surface
- The symbols glow faintly — they are inscribed into the bread, not painted
- Feels: ritual, precise — this is sustenance with intent baked in

### 6. Aether Drops
- Colour: soft blue → near-white (#7B8FE8 → #C8D8FF)
- Three or four perfect spherical droplets, condensed from pure attention
- They float slightly — barely held by gravity
- Feels: light, bright, clean — like drinking clarity

### 7. Shadow Bark
- Colour: charcoal → near-black (#888899 → #222233)
- A gnarled piece of bark from a tree that grows in shadow
- Ancient rings visible in the cross-section — centuries of rings
- Feels: bitter, old, deeply rooted — takes time to work

### 8. Light Petal
- Colour: warm gold → white-gold (#D4AC0D → #FFF8D0)
- A single petal that gives off its own light — like a petal made of captured sunlight
- Semi-transparent, glowing from within
- Feels: rare, opening, tastes like understanding

### 9. Void Wine
- Colour: deep amber → void-black (#E8C76A → #1A0A00)
- A small vial or chalice containing liquid that shifts between amber and absolute dark
- Small void bubbles rising through it
- Feels: transcendent, strange — fermented from nothing

## SECTION 2 — RELICS (8 sprites)

These are earned achievement items displayed in the companion's inventory.
They should feel like objects from a mystery school — artefacts, seals, symbols given form.
More ornate than the food sprites. These are EARNED — they should look it.

### 1. Flame Relic (vigil_flame)
- Colour: amber (#FF6B6B + fire tones)
- A small flame captured inside a glass vial or obsidian seal
- The flame burns eternally — it is the vigil, made material
- Earned: 7-day practice streak

### 2. Seven-Day Mark (streak_7)
- Colour: white-gold (#F0F0E0)
- A small seven-pointed star stamped into a clay token
- Feels like: a seal pressed into wax — a mark of passage

### 3. Month Mark (streak_30)
- Colour: stellar gold (#FFD700)
- A larger star — the seven-day mark's older sibling
- More complex, more layers, more depth to the geometry
- Earned: 30-day streak — feels like the first real milestone

### 4. Century Mark (sovereign_100)
- Colour: sovereign blue-gold (#1A2A5E + #FFD700)
- An ancient coin or medallion — '100' in LAMAGUE script on the face
- The gate that remembers your face
- Earned: 100 dives

### 5. Bicentenary (sovereign_200)
- Colour: deep red + gold (#C0392B + #FFD700) — Rubedo colours
- The highest relic — the most ornate
- A seal of completion: the alchemical completion symbol (⊕) in full relief
- Earned: 200 dives — the Rubedo

### 6. Entropy Slain (entropy_slain)
- Colour: deep violet + white (#7B3FBE + #FFFFFF)
- A broken spiral — entropy's symbol, fractured
- The fracture lines glow with light — you shattered it
- Earned: defeating an entropy entity in battle

### 7. Well Fed (well_fed)
- Colour: warm amber (#E8C76A)
- A small bowl with three different food remnants inside — LAMAGUE symbols floating above it
- Warm, domestic, powerful in a quiet way
- Earned: feeding companion 3 foods in one day

### 8. Full Loadout (gear_full)
- Colour: teal (#4ECDC4)
- Five gear slots shown as small glowing icons arranged in a pentagonal formation
- All five lit — a declaration of full armament
- Earned: all five gear slots equipped

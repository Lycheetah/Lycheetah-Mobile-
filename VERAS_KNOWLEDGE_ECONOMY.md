## I. WHAT VERAS IS

**Veras (✧) is the smallest indivisible unit of knowledge** — what an atom is to matter,
or a satoshi is to value: indivisible, countable, meaningful only because it composes into
something larger. Knowledge itself is the greater *current* — the living flow, the "us" of
the universe.

Veras is not money first. **Veras is proof that knowledge is flowing and is valuable.**

The system exists so that hungry minds — free users included — can learn, create, and
teach, and be rewarded *when what they bring genuinely benefits the School.*

---

## II. THE PRINCIPLE (not the mechanism)

Veras rewards **demonstrated value over time**, never hype, never clout, never purchase.
A contribution earns its place by being genuinely studied and genuinely useful — proven,
not claimed. The reward system is **fair by architecture**: built so that no individual,
group, bot swarm, or coordinated falsification can game its way to a payout.

*How that fairness is achieved — the model that makes it ungameable — is proprietary and
withheld.* What matters publicly is the promise it guarantees: merit is earned by real,
sustained benefit to the whole, and the path to recognition is the same for everyone.

---

## III. THE SOUL OF IT — unveiling hidden teachers ★

This is the real reason Veras exists. It is built to **find the hidden teachers** — the
many minds who carry real insight but stay silent out of shame, imposter syndrome, or fear
of being belittled. The ones who think *"Who am I to teach this?"*

Veras gives them permission:

> *"If people actually study what I bring, the School itself will notice and reward me.*
> *No gatekeepers. No clout required. Just demonstrated value."*

It turns Sovereign Sol into a true merit-based knowledge current where anyone hungry enough
can participate, teach, and be recognised.

---

## IV. THE LINES THAT HOLD (the covenant)

From the Obsidian Covenant — payment never buys a better mind, only standing and rooms:

1. **Veras never gates intelligence.** It rewards contribution; it never throttles learning.
   A free user's Sol is identical to a paying user's Sol.
2. **Earning is by demonstrated benefit, never by purchase.** You cannot buy recognition.
3. **The system is fair by design** — transparent ramp, same path for everyone, no reproach.
4. **Giveback is load-bearing.** A fixed share returns to the commons; the economy feeds
   its makers *and* the School, or it isn't this economy.

## GROK PROMPT TEMPLATE

Paste this at the start of every generation prompt:

> **"High quality pixel art character sprite. RPG sprite style, 32-bit aesthetic. Pure white background, no floor, no shadow on ground. Full body floating figure. Glowing edges, particle effects rendered into the sprite. Portrait orientation. Lycheetah aesthetic: ancient mysticism meets neon digital, techno-pagan energy, quantum glow. [COMPANION-SPECIFIC TEXT BELOW]"**

After generation: drop into `assets/companions/raw/` → run `python3 scripts/remove_bg.py` → transparent PNGs land in `assets/companions/`.

File naming: `[id]_stage1.png` / `_stage2.png` / `_stage3.png`

---

## LYCHEETAH AESTHETIC RULES (apply to all 18)

- Every companion has **neon glow bleeding at pixel edges** — the Lycheetah signature
- Ancient symbology + digital/circuit details coexist in every design
- **Stage 1:** small, raw, unpolished — the potential not yet realized
- **Stage 2:** awake, defined, glowing — the form found
- **Stage 3:** transcendent — partly dissolving into their element, maximum particle FX
- Palette = the skin's color (listed per companion). Glow should match. Black void pixels for shadows.
- Every companion has a **Lycheetah mark** somewhere: a ✧ glyph, circuit trace, or neon spot pattern

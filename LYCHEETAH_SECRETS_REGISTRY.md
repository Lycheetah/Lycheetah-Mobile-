**Unlock:** LYCHEE BLOOM pet
**Core teaching:** The lychee as symbol — rough exterior, translucent interior, seed at center. What is most valuable does not present itself. The rough skin is the first gate. The treasure was never hidden; it was simply inside.
**Seal:** 𝔏 — the fruit that hides is the fruit worth finding

### Secret II — TWO FIRES, ONE FORGE
**Unlock:** SOLVE ET COAGULA halo
**Core teaching:** The Athanor (holds heat) and the Mercury (moves) — neither can forge alone. The gold arises between them in the circulation neither controls. Solve et Coagula as mechanism, not metaphor.
**Seal:** 𝔏 — the gold belongs to neither; it arises between them

### Secret III — THE QUESTION IS THE KEY
**Unlock:** THE CODEX pet
**Core teaching:** The school never answers — only opens. An answer fills and closes a vessel. A question keeps it open. The domains and subjects are scaffolding, not knowledge. The door is inside.
**Seal:** 𝔏 — the school does not give you the answer. it gives you the vessel.

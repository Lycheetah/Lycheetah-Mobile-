## What shipped today (session 2)

### The Name
**Headmaster is now Magister.**

Storage key unchanged (`headmaster`) — no migration needed.

### Mystery School
- **★ SAVED SUBJECTS** — Star any subject in the domain list. A "SAVED SUBJECTS" strip now appears on the school home for instant re-access. Shows session count and domain colour. One tap opens the subject detail.

- **Study again is first-class** — Studied subjects no longer look "done." Stars float them to the top of the domain list.

### Chat — Tools Tray
Every icon in the `···` menu now has a text label beneath it:
`Attach · Camera · Style · Compare · Council · Stacks · Audit · Distill · Sigil · DNA`

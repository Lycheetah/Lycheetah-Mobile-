# Sol by Lycheetah — Grok Art Brief

## Style Guide (apply to ALL pieces)
- Dark background: near-black (#06060E or pure black)
- Transparent/floating subject — centered, no border, no text, no watermark
- Colour palette: gold (#FFD700), amber (#FF8C00), void purple (#6600CC), spectral teal (#00FFCC)
- Feeling: mystical, symbolic, earned, slightly uncanny
- Format: square, ideally 1024×1024 PNG with transparent or black background
- No realistic photography style — lean painterly, glowing, symbolic

## What Makes It Different

**1. Constitutional AI — not a chatbot**
Every companion in Sol runs on the AURA framework: a coherence-scoring system that measures the quality and consistency of thinking over time. The companions don't just respond — they track your reasoning, flag contradictions, and reflect your growth back to you. This is not a wellness chatbot. It is a thinking partner with a formal internal architecture.

**2. The Mystery School**
17+ domains. 190+ subjects. Philosophy, alchemy, Kabbalah, Noetic science, LAMAGUE (a formal grammar of thought developed by the author), Egyptian mysteries, Norse cosmology, Celtic tradition, Sufi mysticism, quantum consciousness, and more. Each domain is a door. Each door has a world behind it. Users don't browse a menu — they explore a living structure.

**3. The Companion System**
Companions are not cosmetic. They are RPG entities with six evolution stages, gear systems, battle mechanics, and zone-specific worlds. Each zone has unique visual art and atmospheric effects. Users build a relationship with a character that reflects their practice. The companion evolves when the user does.

**4. The Covenant**
- Payment never buys a better mind. Every user gets the same Sol.
- No feature may encode reproach for absence. Companions rest, they do not mourn.
- Nothing free becomes paid. The open gate never narrows.
- The intelligence is the product. The rooms are the business model.

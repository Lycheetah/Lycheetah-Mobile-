# KIMI ART BRIEF — Scene Backgrounds
## Sol Mystery School Companion App

**Format for ALL backgrounds:**
- 1024×1024 PNG
- Pixel art style — crisp, detailed, no anti-aliasing blur
- Dark backgrounds — these are night/otherworldly scenes, not daylight
- Each zone needs 2–3 versions (parallax layers: far/mid/near or variation 1/2/3)
- File naming: `zonename.png`, `zonename2.png`, `zonename3.png`
- Drop into: `assets/scenes/`

**Palette principle:** Each zone has its own dominant colour family. Stick to it.
These sit behind companion sprites — they must be atmospheric, not busy.
The companion floats above. The scene is the world they live in.

### norse3.png — THE HALL OF SLAIN
